About Us

NoteRouter’s mission started with our founder, Nick Gough, who has served in the real estate industry for years on multiple boards participating at the local, regional, state and national level. Years of being on both the delivering and receiving end of underwhelming communication turned into a drive to help associations communicate and connect with their members in a more meaningful way, and that drive turned into a company on a mission: NoteRouter.

It’s Time To Level Up Your Engagement

EASY AMS INTEGRATION

Managing multiple databases for communicating is lame. NoteRouter synchronizes with major management systems, like Rapattoni Magic, so that you no longer have to do it manually.

ADVANCED AUTOMATION

Mltiple communication channels in real-time while adhering to members’ individual content and channel preferences may sound complex, but NoteRouter does it all for you, automatically.

SOPHISTICATED TARGETING

Ever wanted to send a message to your affiliates working in a certain zip code that are active volunteers and joined in the last 12 months? With just a few clicks you can now do so much more.
